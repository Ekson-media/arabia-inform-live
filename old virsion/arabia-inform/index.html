Our Mission

Preserving the Arabic Memory. Powering Decisions.

Arabia Inform was established in January 1998 with a mission that had never been attempted at scale: to build a living, growing, permanent digital memory of the Arabic world's intellectual and media output.

At a time when much of the Arab region's media existed only in print or on broadcast airwaves, Arabia Inform began the monumental task of digitizing, structuring, and preserving this knowledge for future generations. Today, the archive is not simply a library ‚Äî it is a living, searchable, AI-ready corpus of Arabic civilization's documented thought.
