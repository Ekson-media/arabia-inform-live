Our Story

From Vision to Civilization-Scale Archive

In 1998, Arabia Inform began with a singular conviction: that the Arabic-speaking world's knowledge — its news, scholarship, broadcast, and intellectual heritage — must be captured, preserved, and made accessible. Not partially. Not selectively. Completely.

Operating from two dedicated processing centers running 24/7, Arabia Inform has never missed a single day of coverage in over 27 years. What began as a media clipping service has become the world's most comprehensive repository of Arabic knowledge — spanning 3+ billion online articles, 280+ million print clips, 120+ million broadcast hours, and 1.7 million academic papers.

Today, Arabia Inform serves governments, universities, research institutions, corporations, and AI companies across the globe — providing the data, intelligence, and historical context that powers decisions, shapes policy, and advances Arabic language technology.

Mission

Preserving the Arabic Memory. Powering Decisions.

To capture, preserve, and provide intelligent access to every meaningful piece of Arabic content published or broadcast — serving those who need data to lead, research, and innovate.

Vision

The World's Definitive Arabic Knowledge Authority

To be the indispensable foundation for Arabic media intelligence, academic research, and AI development — the standard against which all Arabic data is measured.
